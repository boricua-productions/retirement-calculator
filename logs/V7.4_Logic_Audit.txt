P1 — Shielded Mode A documents and codifies T7-before-T8: belt-
tightening fires first; T8 sizes against the *minimum* gap, not
the base gap. The literal P1 invariant remains stricter than the
engine (T8 still fires when the minimum gap exceeds buffers) —
this is intentional and now explicit in README + source comments.

P3 / SCN-B (FIXED in V7.4) — Jido Teate now accrues PER COVERED
MONTH (each bi-monthly payment = rate(prev month) + rate(cur
month)) rather than snapping to the age at payment date. Drift
in transition years is ¥0.

SCN-A (FIXED in V7.4) — Mode B now uses a 4-month buffer-
minimum projection. When either buffer is projected to dip below
50% of its target, Mode B fires a Tier-8 sale sized to restore
BOTH buffers to FULL target (minus next-month dividend look-
ahead). The companion fix to `liquidate_for_jpy_target` forces a
real deficit before calling v7_liquidate_for_deficit so the sale
actually executes regardless of current bridge balance.
